Game Instruction

Keyboard Controls:

Player 1: a to turn left, d to turn right, and w to shoot laser

Player 2: 🠘 to turn left, 🠚 to turn right, and 🠙 to shoot laser


How to play:

Your bike leaves behinds a ray trail as it moves. Control your bike to avoid crashing into the walls, rocks, or trails (including your own). Use the laser to break through rocks, ray trails, or destroy the other bike. The goal is to be the last survivor!
